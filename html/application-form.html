Mosaic Reseller Application

Thank you for your interest in Mosaic Manufacturing! In order to be considered as an official reseller, please submit your information via the form below. Should your responses satisfy our requirements, we will be in contact.

Reseller Qualification Requirements
Please ensure that the below criteria are met before completing an application form:

– Been in operation for at least 1 year
– You are currently reselling 3D printing products
– You have you own sales point (site, network, blog, online business, etc.) and have the infrastructure needed to collect payments from customers
– Have warehousing capabilities

Please note that authorized resellers must place their first order within 30 days of signing on.
